DataSet- casestudydata.csv : It has the data of 8819 adults who has filled a survey about their health conditions 
			     and the Survey was conducted by National Centre for Health Statistics (Centre for Disease Control).



Objective - In these 8819 Adults, 2819 people do not know If they have Chronic kidney Disease(CKD),
	    objective is to predict if they CKD or not based on their information. And after that make a easy to use screening tool to identify CKD.


Model- Logistic Regression
